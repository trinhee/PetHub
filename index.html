About

Pethub operates as an online platform specializing in the sale of pets and pet related products, adopting a place to cater everyones needs. Our website does not only offer a variety of adorable companions but also provides premium pet products! The structure of our website includes an 'about' section, introducing what we have to offer and highlighting our amamzing team. Our 'Products' section show cases all of our products, detailing their specifications. Our 'Contact' section allows for our users to communicate with us regarding problems such as product info, pricing, and more. Lastly, our 'References' section serves as a testament to the platform's dedication, featuring the references to all pictures used.
